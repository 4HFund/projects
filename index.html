Luau Manor Work Order & Complaint Form

Use this form to submit a maintenance work order or file a complaint for your apartment at Luau Manor. Select the appropriate request type below.

Provide the required information about the work needed. Completing all fields helps us respond promptly. Fields marked with * are required.

When requesting maintenance, please describe the work needed as clearly as possible, including the room or area (e.g., kitchen, bathroom) and any specific issues you have noticed. This helps our maintenance team prepare and respond efficiently.

When submitting a complaint, please provide details such as what happened, where and when it occurred, and who was involved. Complaints can include issues related to building conditions, policy violations, or behavior of residents or staff.

If this is an emergency and it is after hours, please call 304‑242‑2820; do not complete this form.
